This is a small site project where I play and write out the first few encounters from the Cyberpunk 2020 adventure book, Home of the Brave. Cyberpunk 2020 is a tabletop RPG system from the late 80s and early 90s not unlike Dungeons & Dragons. The book describes a world in which two to six players, referred to in-universe as Edgerunners, strap on their spike-laden gloves and step into the shoes of individuals inhabiting a dystopian, street cred obsessed future.
