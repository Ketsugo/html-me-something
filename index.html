HTML-Me-Something

This is a small site project where I play and write out the first couple of encounters from the Cyberpunk 2020 adventure book, Land of the Free. Cyberpunk 2020 is a tabletop RPG system from the late 80's and early 90's not unlike Dungeons & Dragons. Instead of using an assortment of polyhedral dice, it uses a streamlined selection of standard d10s and d6s for everything gameplay related. The standard player's handbook describes a world in which a Referee and two to six players, referred to in-universe as Edgerunners, strap on their spike-studded gloves and step into the shoes of diverse individuals inhabiting a dystopian, street cred obsessed future.

Overall, it's mainly a short creative writing project, but I've also gone ahead and written an embedded script, adding simple DOM-based navigation to the bottom of the page to change out the contents as needed.

Introduction

Cyberpunk 2020's America is a much different, fractured thing. Emissions and greenhouse gases have gone unchecked, effectively rendering vast quantities of interior US lands inhospitable by 2020. For lack of a better word, anywhere outside of the big cities is a hellscape. It isn't much better inside of the cities, either, with New York City suffering a nuclear device detonation in the early 90s, leaving most of Manhattan Island in shambles. The Player's Handbook and Home of the Brave sourcebook go into much more detail than I have room for writing here, but the gist of it is that the NSA, CIA, FBI, and DEA 'team up' during a Presidential succession crisis in the mid 90's and attempt to govern from the shadows. The United States we know ceased to exist with some states, wanting more power, seceding shortly after the crisis and damaging the US irreparably.

The Adventure

The big Fixer in the Big Apple, or what's left of it in this version of 2020, is Eliot Weir (he/him). He's gathered a small cell of Edgerunners to run an extraction job - freeing an individual from an unmarked corporate laboratory in New York City and escorting them safely across the country to Night City by air, which is situated on the west coast of the United States... a tremendous urban sprawl planned right between Northern California's San Francisco and Southern California's Los Angeles. It's going to be a tough job, but he's certain that these punks will succeed.

The Punks

Roll (they/them) - A Solo hired gun looking for their first big job after parting ways with their teacher. They found exactly what they had been looking for with Weir's cold-call DataTerm message. After working under one of the west coast's most reputable Solos before they suddenly decided to retire, Roland has some huge boots to step into. Eager to find their place in this war-torn country, they suppress the strange feeling in the pit of their stomach as they cross the threshold of the apartment Weir instructed them to meet at.

Sonja (she/her) - A Nomad finding herself lost and out of her element in the big city after her gang broke up, she sent some feelers out looking for work and Weir's quick response is the first of a few that she had planned on showing up to. Desperate to take herself off the street and back into a bucket seat, she agreed to the extraction job without hesitation.

Jawn (he/him) - A Fixer skilled in the art of the deal, he's informed of Weir's job through the local Big Apple grapevine and reaches out. Having another Fixer on-site would lessen the burden of sourcing everything for this job, so naturally he accepts Jawn's offer. Appreciating how important it is to show up on-time to a deal, he's the first to arrive at the scene on the south side of Manhattan Island.
